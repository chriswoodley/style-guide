The Box Arrow pattern is provided as a mixin rather than predefined css rules. This allows you to pass in the side, color, and width of your arrow and can be applied to any element.

@include box-arrow($side: top, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: top-right, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: right, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: bottom-right, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: bottom, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: bottom-left, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: left, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
@include box-arrow($side: top-left, $color: $blue, $width: $base-font-size);
